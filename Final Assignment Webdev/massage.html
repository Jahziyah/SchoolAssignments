What to Expect

Your massage experience begins the moment you enter our tranquil space. You'll be greeted warmly and offered herbal tea or infused water while completing a brief health questionnaire. Your therapist will discuss your specific needs, preferences for pressure, and any areas of concern. During your session, you'll be in a private, temperature-controlled room with soft lighting and calming music. All linens are premium quality and heated for your comfort. Your therapist will use professional-grade massage oils or lotions, and you can request unscented options if preferred.
