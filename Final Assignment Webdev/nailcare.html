Nail Care Services

Our nail care services are designed to pamper your hands and feet, leaving you feeling refreshed and polished. Whether you're looking for a classic manicure or a luxurious pedicure, our skilled technicians are here to provide exceptional care and attention to detail.

Treatment Options & Benefits

  • Birthday Cake Manicure (40 min - $60): Enjoy a warm hand soak, nail trim and file, cuticle nourishment, relaxing hand massage, exfoliation with a birthday cake scrub and moisturizing, followed by a polish application.
  • Essential Manicure (25 minutes (express without polish) - $50, 40 minutes - $60, 50 minutes (French Manicure) - $65:) Relax and let our expert team take care of your hands with our hand facial service featuring premium Canadian hand-care products. It begins with an aromatic cleanse, a warm hand soak, nail trim and shape, cuticle nourishment, a renewing hand serum followed by and a relaxing massage with Probiotic Hand Balm. The award-winning products are known for their use of premium ingredients and plant-based emollients, ensuring your hands are nourished, clean, hydrated, exfoliated, and protected. End your hand facial with a polish application using products from the Manucurist or OPI collection.
  • Bliss Deluxe Manicure (50 min - $85, 55 min (Deluxe French Manicure)- $90): All the benefits of an Essential manicure, plus a firming masque on hands and arms, plus a paraffin hand treatment.
  • Essential Pedicure (50 min - $169): This nurturing experience not only soothes your body but also creates a tranquil space for you to connect with your growing baby, leaving you feeling refreshed, balanced, and ready to embrace the journey ahead.

What to Expect

Your nail care experience begins with a warm welcome and a choice of herbal tea or infused water. Your technician will discuss your nail preferences and any sensitivities before guiding you to a comfortable, relaxing space. You'll enjoy a soothing soak, gentle shaping, cuticle care, and light exfoliation, followed by a calming hand or foot massage. All tools are professionally sanitized, and our natural products are chosen to nourish and protect your nails. Finish with your choice of polish or a simple natural shine, leaving you refreshed, cared for, and beautifully pampered.
